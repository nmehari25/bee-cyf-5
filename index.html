Travel to Taitung in Taiwan

Top places to visit for

  • Nature

    • Taitung Forest Park - with its beautiful bike trails, hibiscus plants, and a beautiful pond.

  • Adventure

    • Green Island - this small volcanic island is great place for snorkeling, soaking in the Zhaori Hot Springs, or renting a scooter to visit landmarks like the Green Island Lighthouse and the Little Great Wall.

  • Culture

    • Tiehuacun Night Market - best to stroll and enjoy the illuminating lanterns on a Friday night. Also, catch one of the musical events or street art performances.

  • Food

    • Try the sweet and custardy mix of banana and citrus flavors of the locally grown custard apple. Taste desserts such as Ba Bao Fan made from locally grown Jingbao Rice which is sticky and glutinous, perfect for the sweet dessert.

    * Donghe Township - Also has a number of artisinal products and has a lot of local produce on offer.
